Corentin Lunel

I am a PhD student at Laboratoire d'Informatique Gaspard Monge of Université Gustave Eiffel, working under the supervision of Arnaud de Mesmay and Pierre Dehornoy.

I am motivated by topics related to mathematics, in particular low-dimensional topology and theoretical computer science. I am studying graphs and knots from a computational point of view.
